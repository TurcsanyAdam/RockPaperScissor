User stories

Show if an opponent is available or still searching:
Notify the player with a message when an opponent is ready for play

Base game rules should apply:
- rock beats scrissors
- scissors beat paper
- paper beats rock

Pick choice with button:
A button should be available for all 3 choices.

Show the player's pick:
Only the player's pick should be visible, until his opponent made his choice.

Show outcome:
After both players picked for the round, the outcome should be announced for both parties,
with their respective picks.
